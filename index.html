Delaware Area Career Center


DACC provides unique elective courses to high school students in Delaware County and surrounding areas. We work in partnership with your high school to enhance your academic education with hands-on instruction that is focused on each individual student's area of interest. DACC students still graduate from their home high school, but they do so with additional college credits, industry credentials, and valuable experiences.
